Self Love

I am learning to be gentle
With the person in the mirror.
To speak in softer words,
To silence my own fear.
I am not perfect
And I don’t need to be.
Every scar, every flaw
Is still a part of me. 💙

Blooming from Within

I am learning to speak softly,
To the quiet voice inside.
Each scar tells a story,
Each flaw, a lesson to guide.

I do not need perfection,
Nor the world’s applause.
For the love I seek outside
Begins with my own heart’s cause.

Today I choose patience,
Tomorrow, kindness too.
The longest journey I’ll travel
Is the one back to me and you. 💛

Overthinking

Chasing shadows that aren’t there.
Questions multiply like whispers,
Answers vanish into thin air.

I try to catch each worry,
But they slip through my hands.
The more I chase the future,
The less I understand today’s plans.

So I pause, take a breath,
Let the noise softly fade.
In the quiet, I find clarity,
And peace I almost mislaid. 🌿
